Yangzhou City Library

This is a photograph taken at Yangzhou City Library. I was walking around the library and saw the beautiful structure when I looked up in the sky. Actually, the structure itself isnâ€™t very patterned, but is concave and convex at the same time. I tried to find a sweet point of the structure where it looks very ordered. Finally, I found it, shooting it with my Fujifilm X-100f. In the post production period, I cleaned the glass and added some blue color in it.

This super patterned remarkable structure shows the modernness of Yangzhou city. Instead of focusing on the architecture as a whole, I payed my attention to a small part of it, the glass-steel roof. I learned this expressive skill from Lee Frost in his article PHOTOGRAPHING CITIES in BLACK + WHITE.
